Registration

Tickets are available on a first come, first serve basis. The registration is free and covers the catering expenses for the first 100 registrations.

Accomodation

Accomodation is estimated to be 95 EUR per person per day in a shared room (475 EUR / week). Please pre-book your accommodation in the Eventbrite registration form (to be available on May 1st). You will be contacted by hotel directly with the confirmation and information about the payment. Room arrangements will be suggested and notified two weeks before the event.
