Registration

Tickets are available on a first come, first serve basis. The registration is free and covers the catering expenses for the first 100 registrations.

What is included:

  • Full board (breakfast, lunch and dinner)
  • Morning and afternoon snacks
  • Wine with dinner, aperitifs (white wine, sparkling wine, beers)
  • Tea, coffee, cold drinks and refreshments throughout the day
  • A sweet or savoury welcoming bite to eat depending on arrival time
  • Additionally: “open bar” aperitifs (€15 excl. VAT per participant) and party evening (open bar until 2 a.m. €30 excl. VAT per participant)

    • What is excluded:

    The accomodation in shared chalet.

    Register!
    Data protection: The registration form gives you the opportunity to share your contact details with other attendees. If you agree to share your details (name, email, affiliation), these will be kept by the ELIXIR Hub and on EvenBrite for two months after the event. After this time they will be deleted from both locations. If you want the details to be removed earlier, or if you have any queries, please contact us at bh2018paris@elixir-europe.org.
