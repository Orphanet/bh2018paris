--- layout: page title: Registration abstract: Available from May 1st, 2018 ---

Tickets are available on a first come, first serve basis. There is no registration fee but the participants should make their own hotel reservation at the venue. The free registration covers the catering expenses for the first 100 registrations. Accomodation is 95 EUR per person per day in a shared room (475 EUR / week). Please pre-book your accommodation in the registration form. You will be contacted by hotel directly with the confirmation and information about the payment. Room arrangements will be suggested and notified two weeks before the events.

Deadline for registration

September 15th, 2018
